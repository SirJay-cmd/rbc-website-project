About Us

Ruaraka Baptist Church has been a beacon of hope, love, and faith for decades. Our mission is to share the love of Christ and make disciples through worship, service, and outreach.

Our Ministries

Children Ministry

"Growing Hearts for Jesus!"

At Ruaraka Baptist Church, our Children’s Ministry is a vibrant, joyful space where kids learn about God's love in fun and meaningful ways. Through Bible stories, music, crafts, and interactive lessons, we help children build a strong foundation of faith. Our goal is to partner with families to raise up the next generation of Christ-followers in a safe, nurturing, and exciting environment.

"Real Faith. Real Life. Real Connection."

Our Youth Ministry is a place for middle and high school students to explore their faith, build lasting friendships, and discover their purpose in Christ. We meet regularly for worship, teaching, small groups, and events that challenge and inspire. We’re raising leaders who live boldly for Jesus in their schools, homes, and communities.

"Rooted in Christ. Growing Together."

Our Women’s Ministry is a space where women of all ages can connect, grow in faith, and encourage one another. Through Bible studies, retreats, prayer gatherings, and outreach, we support each other in every season of life. Come as you are and find a sisterhood rooted in love, grace, and purpose.

"Strong Faith. Strong Brotherhood."

We believe men thrive when they grow in faith alongside other men. Our Men’s Ministry is about discipleship, accountability, and leadership. Whether it's through Bible studies, breakfasts, service projects, or mentoring, we’re building a band of brothers who seek to honor God in their families, work, and communities.

"Worshiping God. Leading Others."

Our Worship Ministry exists to glorify God through music and creative expression. Whether you're a musician, vocalist, or have a passion for tech and production, there's a place for you to serve. Together, we lead our church into meaningful encounters with God, creating an atmosphere where hearts are transformed in His presence.

"Love in Action."

We are committed to being the hands and feet of Jesus in our community. Through food drives, service projects, partnerships with local organizations, and more, our Outreach Ministry seeks to meet needs and share the hope of Christ. Everyone has something to give—come join us in making a difference!
